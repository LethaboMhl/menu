Our story

Flour, fire, and a lot of patience.

We started in 2018 with one deck oven and a stubborn belief that bread should take its time. Today we mill our own flour, ferment our doughs for 36 hours, and cook over an open hearth. Everything on our menus is made in-house, from the butter on your toast to the ice cream on your tart.
